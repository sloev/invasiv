The lightweight, symmetric powerhouse for modern VJs and projection artists. Sync anything, anywhere, instantly.

Symmetric Architecture

Every instance is identical. Toggle between Master and Peer roles on the fly. No more complex server/client setups—just broadcast and play.

Video-Tempo Skewing

Unique phase-locked loops automatically skew video playback speed to ensure your loops land perfectly on the metronome beats. Frame-accurate rhythmic sync.

Spline Warping

High-performance Catmull-Rom spline surfaces for complex projection mapping. Manage dozens of surfaces across multiple network nodes from a single UI.
