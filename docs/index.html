The Rhythmic Skew

Videos are no longer static loops. INVASIV's skew engine uses Phase-Locked Loops (PLL) to dynamically nudge playback speed.

Your content doesn't just play; it aligns. Frame by frame, beat by beat, your visuals stay locked to the master metronome with sub-millisecond precision.
