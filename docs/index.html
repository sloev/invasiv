INVASIV
Distributed Spatial Intelligence

Visuals should scale with your imagination, not your budget.

Invasiv is a lightweight, C++ powered engine designed for distributed environments. Instead of one massive computer struggling with cables, Invasiv uses multiple affordable nodes—one at every projector—synchronized instantly over a standard network.

Rethinking the Setup

Legacy Setup (Centralized)

One expensive PC + Many long, fragile video cables. Single point of failure. High hardware cost.

Invasiv Setup (Distributed)

Many affordable laptops + Standard Ethernet. Modular, redundant, and scales to any size.

Symmetric Network

Every node is identical. Any computer can be the Master or a Peer. If a machine fails, just swap roles. No more show-stopping crashes.

Smart Beat-Matching

Using our Skewer tool, video events are pinned to a grid. Invasiv micro-adjusts playback speed to ensure frames always hit the beat without jumpy loop restarts.

Architectural Lean

Pure C++20 with zero bloat. The binary is ~20MB. It runs natively on your hardware, making the most of every CPU cycle on even older laptops.
