The Skewer Method

1. Pin the Action

Open raw footage in SKEWER. Mark exactly when significant visual events occur. Pin the action to the timeline, not just the time.

2. Warp the Loop

Export from Skewer. It non-linearly time-stretches your video so every marked event lands on a perfect 2-second grid.

3. Lock the Beat

Load into INVASIV and tap your BPM. The engine micro-adjusts playback speed so your video events stay phase-locked to the pulse.
